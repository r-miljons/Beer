Since 1991 we have been gathering to share our love for this precious golden liquid. What started as a simple gathering of friends has now evolved into a full-fledged club house. Anyone can come and share their own home-brewed goods and learn about the fascinating world of ethanol based liquids. If this feels like a dream come true, send us a message and you too can have a chance of joining our club.
